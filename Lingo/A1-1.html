LESSON

Definite articles in the singular

Learn to use der, die, and das with confidence.

This lesson helps you choose the right article for each noun and recognize common signals for grammatical gender.

Beginner · A1
8 minutes

Learning goals

Focus points for today

  • Match each noun with the correct articleder · die · das
  • Spot common word endings-er · -e · Ge- · -chen
  • Practice common everyday sentencesSpeaking and writing

Article overview

Examples from daily life

  • der MannPeople and many job titles
  • die LampeObjects ending in -e, -heit, -keit
  • das KindNouns with Ge-, -chen, -lein

Memory tips

Strategies for new words

  • Write down the articleWrite every new noun with its article in your notebook.
  • Look for endings-ung, -schaft → often die · -ment, -tum → often das
  • Use imagesLink articles with colors or symbols.
